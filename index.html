Visit Bacolod City

Top three activities to do in Bacolod City

Go on a Food Trip (Chicken Inasal & More!)

When you visit Bacolod, trying chicken inasal is a must. Pair it with rice, calamansi, and chicken oil, then explore local desserts like piaya and napoleones.

Attend the Masskara festival

The MassKara Festival is Bacolod’s most famous celebration, held every October. It’s known for colorful smiling masks, street dancing, loud music, and nonstop parties that really show the city’s joyful spirit.

Visit the Ruins

As one of Bacolod’s top attractions, The Ruins offers a glimpse into the past. Originally the home of Don Mariano Ledesma Lacson, the structure’s beautiful design continues to captivate visitors decades later.
